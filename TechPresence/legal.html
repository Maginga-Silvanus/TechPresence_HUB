About Tech Presence

Tech Presence is a leading freelancer platform dedicated to connecting talented professionals with businesses and individuals seeking quality services. We operate as a digital marketplace that bridges the gap between service providers and clients globally.

Who We Are

Company Name: Tech Presence

Business Type: Freelancer Marketplace Platform

Mission: To empower freelancers and enable businesses to find top talent for their projects

Vision: To become the most trusted freelancer platform that fosters innovation, collaboration, and professional growth

Our Commitment

We are committed to:

  • Providing a safe and secure platform for freelancers and clients
  • Ensuring fair payment practices and dispute resolution
  • Maintaining high standards of professionalism and quality
  • Protecting user data and privacy
  • Supporting skill development through training programs
  • Rewarding loyalty through our referral program

Our Services

Tech Presence offers a comprehensive range of online services to connect freelancers with opportunities:

1. Job Marketplace

Browse and apply to thousands of online job opportunities from global clients. Our platform features diverse projects ranging from web development, graphic design, content writing, digital marketing, and many more.

2. Training Programs

Enhance your skills with our professional training programs designed by industry experts. Participate in live training sessions, acquire certifications, and improve your portfolio to attract better-paying projects.

3. Payment Processing

Secure payment methods with support for multiple banks and payment options. We ensure timely and secure payment transfers for completed work.

4. Referral Program

Earn additional income by referring friends and colleagues to Tech Presence. Get 10% commission on every successful referral while your friends enjoy a 5% discount on their first project.

5. Profile Management

Build a professional portfolio, showcase your work, and connect with potential clients. Our profile tools help you highlight your expertise and attract quality projects.

Note: All services are provided digitally and can be accessed from anywhere in the world. Tech Presence operates 24/7 to support our global freelancer community.

Privacy Policy

Tech Presence collects the following information:

  • Financial Information: Bank details, payment method, transaction history

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:
